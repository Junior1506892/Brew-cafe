Our Story

From bean to brew - our journey of passion

Our Mission

To create a community space where exceptional coffee, genuine connections, and sustainable practices come together. We believe every cup should tell a story of quality, ethics, and craftsmanship.

Founded in 2018 by Alex Morgan, Brew & Bean started as a small kiosk with a big dream: to bring specialty coffee to our neighborhood while supporting ethical farming practices.
